FAQ

Questions, answered

How does battery health prediction work? +
Models ingest charging cycles, temperature, usage patterns and fast-charge frequency to output a health score, remaining useful life and a replacement risk band.
Can VoltMind integrate with our existing telematics? +
Yes — VoltMind ingests standard telematics and charger protocols and layers AI intelligence on top without replacing your hardware.
Is our fleet and supplier data secure? +
Role-based access, encrypted storage and audit trails are built in, with optional on-prem / VPC deployment for enterprise customers.
Do you support multi-OEM fleets? +
Yes — procurement intelligence and fleet monitoring both work across mixed OEM fleets including Tata, Mahindra, Ashok Leyland and Volvo Eicher.
